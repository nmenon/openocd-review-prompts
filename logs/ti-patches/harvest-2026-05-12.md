```tcl

    jtag newtap $_CHIPNAME cpu -irlen 4 -expected-id $_K3_DAP_TAPID -ignore-version
```

NOT the manual `if {[using_jtag]}/else` pattern. `swj_newdap` encapsulates the transport
selection logic correctly and works with all transports including SWD multi-drop.

**Rules derived:** TCL-16 (gdb-attach must preserve halt), TCL-17 (consider telnet users).
